Proof of Life

(01) Origin of Life

Starting with basic particles floating around a given space, all lifeforms originate through basic building blocks that assemble a larger being. Particles are the building blocks of matter and life because they form atoms, which combine to create all substances and living organisms.

Atoms are the smallest units that define an element's properties that interact to form molecules, which are the foundation of everything.

(02) Genetics

Now that we have established how two life particles can join together, we understand the basics of the building blocks of life. In a more abstract sense, these particles can (skip a few steps… and) be organized to create structures and begin to build more complex organisms.

Nucleotides are the building blocks of DNA because they are the single units that link together to form the DNA helix. In a larger, more abstract sense, life can be seen as a series of complex puzzles where nucleotides act as the individual, unique pieces, with their specific sequence forming the code that dictates how these "puzzles" assemble and function.
