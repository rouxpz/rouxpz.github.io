Home Crazy Angel

When you adopt a dachshund from a breeder or a shelter you should know what kind of personalities they can have. They can be a crazy dog who likes to eat rocks and mulch or a perfect little angel who just side eyes you all the time. Whether you prefer one or another they both will side eye you regardless of if you are petting them or forget to feed them by one millisecond.

If you want to support babies that do not have a home or family I have some links that you can check out. If you are in the position to foster animals please do so they can have a quality of life. You can go to Muddy Paws that is based out of New York. You can also go to Best Friends that has places all over the country. Both of these places are a no kill shelter becasue that is very important to me.
