Crazy and her personality

Home Crazy Angel

Crazy aka LuLu is a Dachshund that is absolutely insane. I have personally babysat her before by myself. She was in fact insane. While having a crazy dog can be fun it is a lot of work. I do not take care of her full time but when I do I feel for my boyfriend's parents.

While being crazy she is a baby. She is less than a year old so it is normal for her to be insane. But not to the point where she jumps off the couch and lands on her noggin. Yes you heard that right she lands on her head.

In all seriousness she cuddles up to you and when normal she is very cute. I will share some cute photos but also some where she is in her own world. She does torture her brother who I will talk about and show you how he hides from her insane behavior.
