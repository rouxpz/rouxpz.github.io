Lily of the Valley

Our cold process soap is handcrafted in small batches and is made with simple ingredients. Our mild recipe always includes lots of cocoa and shea butters to gently cleanse your skin.

Ingredients

Oil, Water, Coconut Oil, Shea Butter, Rice Bran Oil, Lye, Cocoa Butter, Castor Oil, Fragrance Oil, Citric Acid, Kaolin Clay, Sodium Lactate, Cane Sugar, Color

4 1/2 ounces, hand-cut
