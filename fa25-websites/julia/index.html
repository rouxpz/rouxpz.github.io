The Everyday Edit

The simple parts of our routines are often overlooked. Yet, small moments like making your morning coffee can simply become some of the most important parts of your day. Whether your day-to-day life follows the exact same routine or just shares a few habits, having routine is one of the most beneficial things a person can do. So think deeper, pay attention to the simple parts of your routine and how it benefits your day. What would you do without your morning coffee or your afternoon walk? How would your day feel without that morning greeting from your dog or cat?
