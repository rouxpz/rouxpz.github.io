The Unified Empire of Aveyron

The mightiest power in all the lands, the fastest developing empire, the hub of world advancement. Those are only some aspects of the Aveyron Empire. Initially founded by elves as a kingdom on the Aveyron Peninsula, this nation has taken over a large portion of the northern continent. From their roots in the forested peninsula, the Aveyronnais have been conquerors of nature, as well as conquerors of culture. Their arcane, technological, social, and military advancement has established the Unified Empire of Aveyron as the first true global superpower.
