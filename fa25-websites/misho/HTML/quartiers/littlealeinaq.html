Little Aleinaq

In the last century, trade between Aveyron and Majjat has strengthened their economic and cultural ties. Traders and merchants spend a lot of time travelling between the two nations, resulting in a growing Majjat population in this quartier named after the nation’s capital, Khalij Aleinaq. Additionally, many aristocrats from Khalij Aleinaq have made Stennopolis their home for its business opportunities.
