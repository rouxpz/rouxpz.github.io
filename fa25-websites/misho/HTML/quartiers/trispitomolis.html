Trispitomolis

This is one of the most densely populated quartiers of Stennopolis, as well as the oldest. The halfling population of Trispitomolis has developed a distinct architectural style, with short, colorful blocks lining the streets in a tight web. Though most of the current city layout was constructed after Aveyronnais settlement, some structures in this quartier date back to Ancient Kendrisia.
