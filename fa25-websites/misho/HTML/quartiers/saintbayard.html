Saint Bayard

Generally considered the most affluent, prosperous, and safe quartier of Stennopolis, Saint Bayard is somewhat sheltered from the hustle and bustle of city life. The population, predominantly upper-class Elven Aveyronnais, tend to isolate themselves from the lower classes or foreign commoners. This area is admired by visitors and locals alike for its ornate, ostentatious architecture, many buildings ornamented in delicate sculptures and precious metals.
