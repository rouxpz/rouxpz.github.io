Nimfaia

The island of Nimfaia is one of the oldest standing settlements in the world. Despite the island’s small size and the nearly untouched ,dense forest, its population is not small. The Nimfaians are a respected people, who reside in the treetops of the forest. Although they have not taken a stance in any major political conflicts in centuries, their reputation as fierce warriors persists. As a result, Aveyron maintains peace with them and Nimfaia is considered a semi-sovereign state. Today, the people of this island are closed-off to most outsiders, dedicating their lives to preserving their tradition, history, and martial arts.
