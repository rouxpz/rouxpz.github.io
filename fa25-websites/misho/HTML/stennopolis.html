Stennopolis

Founded some three millennia ago by dwarves and halflings of Ancient Kendrisia, long before Aveyron conquered this land, Stennopolis is a city rich in history, culture, and conflict. Tensions between people of Kendrisian descent and the Aveyronnais have remained present through centuries of imperial rule, and are reflected in many aspects of Stennopolis society. As a revolution of industry has swept the world over the last century, the City of Stennopolis has become the heart of trade, innovation, and cultural interaction. Eyes were drawn to the city, and over a few short decades it has been redefined as a melting pot of culture, a hub for technological advancement, and a land of opportunity for all. Stennopolis is now among the biggest metropolitan areas in the world, with ethnic populations migrating from Majjat, Levnia, and Albire.
